About Me

Sheep Celica represents just me - Sheep. I own a 5th gen Celica and love pop-up headlights in general. I plan to design and produce several aftermarket parts for the T18 Celicas. Mostly electronics and interior stuff. But who knows, maybe I will do some more in the future.

Who I Am

I was born and live in Czechia 🇨🇿. Currently working full-time as a System Design Engineer for Electron Microscopes.

My skills are mostly self-taught. I studied Mechatronics in Highschool, but thats about it for my education. Programming has been my hobby since ~12 years old.

3D printing and modelling is something that became my hobby as I gradutated Highschool. I actually hated cars as a kid. That changed in Highschool as I came to like the Initial D series. This is also when I found out about pop-up headlight cars!

Over the years I learned how to fix cars myself. I have done a lot of work with the braking system and the suspension. Once I have a proper garage I want to tear down the engine too!

Oh and some of you might be curious about the PCBs. That is another self-taught skill that came to be because of the pop-up controller project. My PCB designs are made by me. This includes the schematics, layuout and routing.
