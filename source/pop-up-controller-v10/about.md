# About Me

Published page: `/products/pop-up-controller-v10/about/`

This page introduces the person behind Sheep Celica for customers who want a quick overview of who
is building the project and what is planned next.

## About Me Description

- Sheep Celica represent just me - Sheep. I own a 5th gen Celica and love pop-up headlights in
  general. I plan to design and produce several aftermarket parts for the T18 Celicas. Mostly
  electronics and interior stuff. But who knows, maybe I will do some more in the future.

## Who I Am

- `24` years old and born in `Czechia` `🇨🇿`.
- Currently working full-time as a `System Design Engineer for Electron Microscopes`.
- Most skills are self-taught.
- Studied `Mechatronics` in high school, but that is about it for formal education.
- Programming has been a hobby since around `12` years old.
- `3D` printing and modeling became a hobby after graduating high school.
- Cars were actually something I disliked as a kid.
- That changed in high school after getting into the `Initial D` series.
- That was also when the interest in pop-up headlight cars started.

## Job Card
