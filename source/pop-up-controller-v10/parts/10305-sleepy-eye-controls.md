## Card Summary

Controls for toggling and adjusting the Sleepy Eye mode positions

## Details

Small-slot panel with a button to toggle Sleepy Eye mode and a switch to adjust the sleepy eye pop-up position.
Supports various LED colors.  
This panel can fit in any of the four small slots in the car.
NOTE The two small slots under the steering wheel may not be suitable for all installations.

## Fact Cards

- Use: Pop-up Sleepy Eye mode control
- Works with: `10322` Sleepy Eye Controls Cable and the controller's 'X5' input.
- Install style: Accessory panel control for compatible interior mounting.
- Compatibility: V8 and V10 Controllers
