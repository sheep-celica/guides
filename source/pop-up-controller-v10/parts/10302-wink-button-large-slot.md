## Card Summary

Large-slot panel with three wink buttons, available in two variants.

## Details

Installs in the large slot next to the parking brake and provides three wink buttons.  
It comes with either my custom 3D-printed buttons or custom AliExpress buttons.  
Supports various LED colors.  

## Fact Cards

- Use: Pop-up wink control
- Works with: `10321` Wink Button Cable and the controller's wink inputs 'X1' to 'X4'.
- Install style: Accessory panel control for compatible interior mounting.
- Compatibility: V8 and V10 Controllers
